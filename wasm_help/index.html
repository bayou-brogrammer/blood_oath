BloodOath

by Jacob LeCoq

BloodOath is my fun little side project

You have harnessed the power of blood magic and must continue to harnest your power or the magic will consume you. Blood, blood, blood is your only goal. Cosume your prey, build your spells, and consume the world, but be careful. Blood Magic is forbidden in the kingdom. If you are caught, the only choice is to the death.

Move around with WASD. Diagonal movement is allowed with YUBN.

You can mouse-over anything to see its description.
